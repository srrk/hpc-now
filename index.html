Why this exists

HPC knowledge should not live only inside vendor ecosystems and research labs — it belongs to the engineers who operate the infrastructure.
Infrastructure that is well understood becomes infrastructure that behaves. The gap between the two is almost always a knowledge problem, not a hardware problem.
Practitioner-built means built by someone who has operated what they teach — not surveyed it, not consulted on it, but run it in production under pressure.
